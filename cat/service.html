Our Services

A student portal application typically offers a range of services to students to enhance their educational experience and streamline administrative tasks. Here are some common services provided by student portal applications:

  • Academic Services:

    Access to course materials, lecture notes, and study resources.
    Course registration, including adding, dropping, and swapping courses.
    Viewing class schedules, exam timetables, and academic calendars.
    Academic advising and support services.
    Access to grades, transcripts, and academic records.
